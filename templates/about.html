Our Mission

We, as we have all see, covid has locked everyone indoors, leaving everyone, with little to no options to get outside. This is where our project, XMarksTheSpot comes in. Using XMarksTheSpot, find numerous places that previously were only known to small groups of people. These cool and beautiful spots are specifically selected to be a place you can venture out to and do whatever it is you want, whether that is to take photos or just look around! So get out there and add cool spots that you can find!
